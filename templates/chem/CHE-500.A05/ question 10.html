Question 10

 

Acetic acid, CH3COOH, is naturally present in vinegar; it is an antiseptic and a disinfectant. It dissociates in water according to the following equation:

 

CH3COOH(aq)  H+(aq) + CH3COO-(aq)          Ka = 1.74 ´ 10-5 at 25°C

 

A solution of acetic acid with a concentration of 0.1 mol/L has a pH of 2.88 at 25°C.

 

What is the concentration of hydroxide ions, OH-, in the solution?

 

A)     1.32 ´ 10-3 mol/L

 

B)     1.74 ´ 10-5 mol/L

 

C)     7.58 ´ 10-12 mol/L

 

D)     1.32 ´ 10-17 mol/L
