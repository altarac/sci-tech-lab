Question 1

 

Gases are used in a variety of areas of human activity. Here are a few examples:

 

1.      In the field of aviation, inflating tires

2.      Cooking on the barbeque

3.      Filling balloons used in meteorology

4.      Fighting blazes with fire extinguishers

 

Associate each of these uses with one of the gases listed below.

 

a.      Neon, Ne

b.      Nitrogen, N2

c.      Carbon Dioxide, CO2

d.      Propane, C3H8

 

A)     1a, 2d, 3b and 4c

 

B)     1b, 2c, 3d and 4a

 

C)     1b, 2d, 3a and 4c

 

D)     1c, 2b, 3a and 4d
