Question 17

 

In order to measure the molar heat of neutralization of a solution of potassium hydroxide, KOH, by a solution of hydrofluoric acid, HF, Bianca performs the following experiment.

 

She pours 100 mL of 0.150 mol/L of KOH solution initially at 22.0°C into 150 mL of 0.100 mol/L of HF solution that is also at 22.0°C.

 

After mixing the two solutions, she notes that the temperature of the mixture rises to 28.0°C.

 

Calculate the molar heat of neutralization of KOH from this data.
