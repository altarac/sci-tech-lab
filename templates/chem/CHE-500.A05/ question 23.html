N2(g) + 3 H2(g)  2 NH3(g) + Energy

-        N2(g)    = 1.0 mol

-        H2(g)    = 0.70 mol

-        NH3(g) = 25 mol

 

a)       What is the equilibrium constant, Kc, of this reaction at 127°C?

 

b)       What is the most probable value of the equilibrium constant, Kc, if the temperature at which the reaction occurs increases to 400°C?
