Question 11

 

According to the regulations of the International Football Association Board (IFAB), the soccer ball must have a pressure between 60.8 kPa and 111.4 kPa for it to be approved.

 

When the ball was prepared for a game, the conditions were:

 

T = 21°C, P = 100 kPa, V = 4.8 L

 

At the start of the game, the temperature was -12°C.

 

Will the referee allow this ball to be used? Explain your answer.
