Question 2

 

Two bottles of gas with the same volume were filled under the same conditions of temperature and pressure. One contains oxygen, O2, and the other nitrogen, N2. Both bottles were opened at the same time.

 

Which of the following statements is TRUE?

 

A)     The bottle containing nitrogen empties more quickly because its molar mass is smaller than that of oxygen.

 

B)     The bottle containing oxygen empties more quickly because its molar mass is larger than that of nitrogen.

 

C)     The two bottles of gas empty at the same time because they contain the same number of molecules of gas.

 

D)     The two bottles of gas empty at the same time because the two gases have the same kinetic energy.
