Question 10

 

Global warming is an environmental concern today.

 

 

Propane, C3H8, is used as an alternative to gasoline, C8H18, as it contributes less to the carbon emissions.

 

The following equation represents the combustion reaction of propane:

 

C3H8(g) + 5 O2(g) ® 3 CO2(g) + 4 H2O(g)

 

Given the following heat of formation reactions:

3 C(s) + 4 H2(g) ® C3H8(g)                  DH = -103.7

H2(g) +  O2(g) ® H2O(g)                   DH = -242.0

C(s) + O2(g) ® CO2(g)                                        DH = -393.5

 

Determine the amount of energy per mole of carbon dioxide produced by the combustion of propane.
