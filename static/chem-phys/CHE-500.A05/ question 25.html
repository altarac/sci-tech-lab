Silver sulfate, Ag2SO4, a sparingly soluble salt, is an ionic compound used in silver plating. It is prepared by adding sulfuric acid, H2SO4, to a solution of silver nitrate, AgNO3, according to the following equation:

 

Ag2SO4(s)  2 Ag+(aq) + SO42-(aq)

 

Given that its solubility product constant, Ksp, is 1.4 ´ 10-5, determine the solubility in g/L of this sparingly soluble salt.
