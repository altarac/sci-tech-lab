Question 3

 

Two gas cylinders of the same volume are exposed to the same conditions of temperature and pressure. One contains propane gas, C3H8, and the other butane gas, C4H10.

 

Which of the following statements is TRUE?

 

A)     Both cylinders contain the same mass of gas.

 

B)     Both cylinders contain the same number of moles of gas.

 

C)     There are more molecules of propane than molecules of butane.

 

D)     There are more molecules of butane than molecules of propane.
