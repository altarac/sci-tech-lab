The nitrogen cycle is an important nutrient cycle that occurs in ecosystems. During the nitrogen cycle, the nitrogen that is in the atmosphere is converted to a form that can be used by plants.

 

During the nitrogen cycle, nitrogen dioxide, NO2, is formed from atmospheric nitrogen gas, N2, and oxygen gas, O2. The balanced chemical equation for the formation of nitrogen dioxide is shown below:

 

N2(g) + 2 O2(g) ® 2 NO2(g)
