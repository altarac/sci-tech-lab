1.      When Natasha goes camping, she always brings her inflatable mattress with her. This year, instead of camping in the summer, she went during the autumn when the temperatures were significantly lower.

 

While inflating her mattress with an air pump, she noticed that it took longer to inflate in the autumn.

 

According to the Kinetic Molecular Theory of gases, which of the following statements best explains why it takes longer to inflate the mattress during the autumn than in the summer?

 

A)     The higher the temperature the faster the air particles move, inflating the mattress at an increased rate.

 

B)     The higher the temperature the faster the air particles move, inflating the mattress at a decreased rate.

 

C)     The lower the temperature the faster the air particles move, inflating the mattress at an increased rate.

 

D)     The lower the temperature the slower the air particles move, inflating the mattress at a decreased rate.
