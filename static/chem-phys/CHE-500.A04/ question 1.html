Question 1

 

Air is a common term for the atmosphere. This layer of nitrogen, oxygen and other trace gases that surrounds our planet makes life on Earth possible.

 

 

The main gases found in air are listed below.

 

N2         O2         H2O          H2          CO2

 

Which of the following combination ranks these gases from the fastest to slowest in ability to diffuse?

 

A)    CO2, O2, N2, H2O, H2

 

B)    CO2, H2O, O2, N2, CO2

 

C)    H2, N2, H2O, O2, CO2

 

D)    H2, H2O, N2, O2, CO2
