The first platform has two jugglers. The second platform, travelling behind the first, carries a cannon which shoots foam balls at the jugglers. The cannon launches the balls at a speed of 15 km/h relative to the platform.

 

At the same time, an acrobat moves horizontally on a cable at 30 km/h in the same direction as the platforms.
