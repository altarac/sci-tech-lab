Question 12

 

Danielle works as a waitress in a restaurant. She is asked to store an open bottle of wine.

 

The bottle of wine contains approximately 58 g of ethanol. If the bottle is left open, the ethanol changes into acetic acid which causes the wine to taste sour.

 

The following reaction illustrates this change:

 

C2H5OH(l) + O2(g) ® CH3COOH(l) + H2O(l)

 

In order to save the wine that is left in the opened bottle, Danielle seals the bottle and places it upright (not on its side) in a cool place.

 

Using collision theory, explain how these two factors slow the reaction.
