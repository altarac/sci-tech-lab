Question 7

 

Ammonia (NH3) is manufactured through the Haber process. In this reaction, nitrogen (N2) gas is combined with hydrogen (H2) gas at 400°C and 202 kPa. The following reaction occurs:

 

N2(g) + 3 H2(g)  2 NH3(g)  ∆H = -92 kJ/mol

 

A leak occurred which resulted in a pressure and temperature drop in the system. The pressure dropped to 101 kPa and the temperature decreased to 325°C.

 

Which of the following would be the MOST IMMEDIATE effect on the reaction?

 

A)    The products would be favoured.

 

B)    The production of ammonia would decrease.

 

C)    ∆H of the reaction would increase.

 

D)    The concentration of nitrogen gas would decrease.
